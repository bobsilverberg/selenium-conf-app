Driving Directions


From the airports:

From Heathrow Airport – Take the eastbound Piccadilly line to Covent Garden. Exit the station onto James Street and turn right towards Covent Garden. Cross Covent Garden and turn right onto Southampton Street. Carry on down Southampton Street onto the Strand. Turn right in Savoy St and walk until you hit Savoy Pl.

From Gatwick Airport – Take the Gatwick Express to Victoria mainline station. Walk to the underground station which is in front of the mainline station and take the Circle or District line to Embankment. From Embankment (Circle, District, Northern and Bakerloo lines), exit the station and proceed up Villiers Street to the Strand. Turn right on the Strand, and continue until Savoy St. Turn right and walk until you hit Savoy Pl.

From Stansted Airport – Take the Stansted Express to Liverpool Street. From Liverpool Street underground station, take the westbound Central line to Holborn and change on to the westbound Piccadilly line, alighting at Covent Garden. Exit the station onto James Street and turn right towards Covent Garden. Cross Covent Garden and turn right onto Southampton Street. Carry on down Southampton Street onto the Strand. Turn right in Savoy St and walk until you hit Savoy Pl.

From Luton Airport – Take the shuttle bus to Luton mainline station. Heading south, alight at King’s Cross and follow the signs to the underground station, which is about 100 metres from the mainline station. Take a westbound Piccadilly line train to Covent Garden. Exit the station onto James Street and turn right towards Covent Garden. Cross Covent Garden and turn right onto Southampton Street. Carry on down Southampton Street onto the Strand and turn left. Turn right in Savoy St and walk until you hit Savoy Pl.

From London City Airport – Take the DLR to Canning Town and change to the Jubilee line, Westbound direction. Upon arriving at Waterloo station, change onto the Northern line and head north alighting at Charing Cross. Exit the station onto the Strand follow Savoy St and walk until you hit Savoy Pl.

From Mainline Train Stations:

From Kings Cross – Take the westbound Piccadilly line to Covent Garden. Exit the station onto James Street and turn right towards Covent Garden. Cross Covent Garden and turn right on to Southampton Street. Carry on down Southampton Street onto the Strand and turn left. Turn right in Savoy St and walk until you hit Savoy Pl.

From Liverpool Street – Take the westbound Central line to Holborn and change on to the westbound Piccadilly line, alighting at Covent Garden. Exit the station onto James Street and turn right towards Covent Garden. Cross Covent Garden and turn right on to Southampton Street. Carry on down Southampton Street onto the Strand and turn left. Turn right in Savoy St and walk until you hit Savoy Pl.

From Charing Cross - Exit the station onto the Strand follow Savoy St and walk until you hit Savoy Pl.

From Waterloo – Take the Northern line, Northbound direction, to Charing Cross station. Exit the station onto the Strand follow Savoy St and walk until you hit Savoy Pl.

From Victoria - Walk to the underground station, which is in front of the mainline entrance. Take the Circle or District line to Embankment. From Embankment (Circle, District, Northern and Bakerloo lines), exit the the station and proceed up Villiers Street to the Strand. Turn right on the Strand, and continue until Savoy St. Turn right and walk until you hit Savoy Pl.

From Paddington – On the underground, take the Bakerloo line southbound to Charing Cross. Exit the station onto the Strand follow Savoy St and walk until you hit Savoy Pl.

From Euston – On the underground, take the Northern line southbound to Charing Cross (please note that the underground branches at Euston and there are two Southbound lines). Exit the station onto the Strand follow Savoy St and walk until you hit Savoy Pl.

By Underground:

From Charing Cross (Northern and Bakerloo lines) – Exit the station onto the Strand follow Savoy St and walk until you hit Savoy Pl.

From Covent Garden (Piccadilly line) – Exit the station onto James Street and turn right towards Covent Garden. Cross Covent Garden and turn right onto Southampton Street. Carry on down Southampton Street onto the Strand. Turn right in Savoy St and walk until you hit Savoy Pl.

From Embankment (Circle, District, Northern and Bakerloo lines) – Exit the station and proceed up Villiers Street to the Strand. Turn right on the Strand, and continue until Savoy St. Turn right and walk until you hit Savoy Pl.
